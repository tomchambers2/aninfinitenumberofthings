YOU WHAT MATE?

What is this monstrosity?

This page generates an infinite number of pseudo life goals using a markov chain algorithm. It picks a sequence of words based on what is statistically likely to occur. Sometimes it will be nonsense, sometimes it will be amusing. It will rarely be less specific and meaningful than the real goals they are based on.

The database of 'things' is made up of real bucket list items collected from the internet. If you want to contribute to the base list of things you can make a pull request on the github page.

It was made by Tom Chambers, a man who has achieved pitifully few of his life goals and is afraid of dying. If you want to hire me for front end development work, you can check out my portfolio website where you can see other things I've done.

Death is a major milestone. Many people say on their deathbed that they wish they'd managed to do ∞ things while they had the chance.

To this end we've compiled a handy list of things you probably ought to do before you shuffle off.
